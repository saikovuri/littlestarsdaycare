🌅 Arrival & Free Play — Soft toys & sensory play (14–24 mo), blocks & pretend play (2–3 yrs), cooperative play & puzzles (3+ yrs).
9:20 – 10:00 AM
📖 Circle Time — Songs & actions (14–24 mo), colors & simple concepts (2–3 yrs), calendar, weather & discussion (3+ yrs).
10:00 – 10:30 AM
🍽️ Morning Snack — Encouraging self-feeding (14–24 mo), spoon/fork practice (2–3 yrs), independent eating & clean-up (3+ yrs).
10:30 – 11:00 AM
🧩 Learning Centers / Activities — Stacking & sensory play (14–24 mo), sorting & fine motor (2–3 yrs), pre-writing, STEM & art centers (3+ yrs).
11:00 – 11:30 AM
🌳 Outdoor Play — Walking & push toys (14–24 mo), running games & slides (2–3 yrs), group games & obstacle course (3+ yrs).
11:30 – 12:00 PM
🧼 Diaper/Restroom & Calm Transition — Diaper change & books (14–24 mo), restroom routine (2–3 yrs), hand wash & quiet reading (3+ yrs).
12:00 – 12:30 PM
🍽️ Lunch Time — Encouraging self-feeding (14–24 mo), eating with guidance (2–3 yrs), independent eating & helping clean (3+ yrs).
12:30 – 2:30 PM
😴 Nap / Rest Time — Nap (14–24 mo), nap or rest (2–3 yrs), quiet rest with books & quiet toys (3+ yrs).
2:30 – 2:45 PM
🧼 Wake Up & Care Routine — Diaper change (14–24 mo), restroom (2–3 yrs), restroom & transition (3+ yrs).
2:45 – 3:05 PM
🍪 Afternoon Snack — Self-feeding practice (14–24 mo), social snack time (2–3 yrs), independent snack & conversation (3+ yrs).
3:05 – 3:30 PM
🎨 Art / Sensory Activities — Simple crafts (14–24 mo), guided crafts (2–3 yrs), creative projects (3+ yrs).
3:30 – 4:15 PM
🏠 Free Play (Indoor/Outdoor) — Exploration play (14–24 mo), pretend play (2–3 yrs), social & group play (3+ yrs).
4:15 – 4:40 PM
📖 Story Time & Music — Short stories (14–24 mo), interactive stories (2–3 yrs), story discussion & songs (3+ yrs).
4:40 – 5:00 PM
👋 Quiet Play & Pick-Up — Books & soft toys (14–24 mo), puzzles & drawing (2–3 yrs), calm activities (3+ yrs). Parents arrive by 5:00 PM.

Flexible & Consistent

While we follow a structured routine, we adapt to each child's needs and energy levels throughout the day.

🌦️ *Outdoor activities are weather permitting. In case of rain or extreme temperatures, we substitute with indoor gross-motor play.

🚽 *We constantly encourage children to use the restroom outside of scheduled times as needed. Potty breaks are never restricted to the schedule — every child is supported whenever they need to go.
